FAQs
Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time developer?
Good question! For starters, the annual cost of a full-time senior-level developer now exceeds $120,000, plus benefits (and good luck finding one available). Aside from that, you may not always have enough work to keep them busy at all times, so you're stuck paying for time you aren't able to utilize. With any plan, you can choose any feature, complicated or not (doesn't matter), as often as you need to ensure you're only paying your application when you have work available for them.
Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?
Once subscribed, you're able to add as many requests to your queue as you'd like, and they will be delivered one by one.
How fast will I receive my software?
On average, most requests are completed in just 31 days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.
What stacks do you code in?
Most requests are designed using the stack selected by the client.
How do I request a software?
Tropical Tech offers a ton of flexibility in how you request software using Trello. Some common ways clients request designs is directly via Trello, sharing Google docs or project scopes, or even recording a brief Loom video (for those who prefer not to write their briefs out). Basically, if it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it's fair game.
What if I don't like the result?
No worries! We'll continue to revise the code until you're 100% satisfied.
What if I only have a single request?
That's fine. You can pause your subscription when finished and return when you have additional requests needs. There's no need to let the remainder of your subscription go to waste.
Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?
Due to the high quality nature of the work, there will be no refunds issued.
